Accomplishments

What makes university a magical time are the brilliant people you meet along the way, and the ground-breaking projects you get to be a part of. I was fortunate to find myself among the folks of UAV Concordia, designing and improving drones for competition in Canada and the United States. This was where my ideas took flight and the sky suddenly was no longer the limit. My sweat and tears were rewarded with accomplishments and memories that I will never forget.

3rd Place in Canada in 2016

Despite having been around for only 3 years at that point, we went shoulder to shoulder with the big names in the game and snatched up 3rd place in all of Canada.

First-of-its-kind R&D Project in North America

Not only were we pushing the boundaries of what already existed, we also wanted to create what didn't. Thus, born the Spherical Drone, the first-of-its-kind in North America.

Ranked 16th Internationally in 2019

Yes yes, I know, ranking 16th technically doesn't sound much like an accomplishment, but considering we ranked 16th out of 75 teams from all around the world, it was pretty darn amazing in my humble opinion.
